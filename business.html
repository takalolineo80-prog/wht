Business Model

💰 Revenue Streams

Elegant Moments Gifts generates income through the sale of gift packages and decoration services.

🎁 Our Offerings

The business offers products such as gift boxes, flower arrangements, and personalized gifts, which are sold directly to customers for different special occasions.

🛍️ Customer Orders & Customization

Customers place orders based on their preferences, and the business provides customized products to meet their needs.

🎉 Decoration Services

In addition, income is earned through decoration services for events such as birthdays, weddings, and romantic setups.

📈 Growth & Strategy

The business also benefits from increased sales during special occasions like Valentine’s Day and birthdays, while maintaining affordable pricing and high-quality service to attract and retain customers.
